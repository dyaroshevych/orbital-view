Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I get my roof assessed?

As a property owner, it is important to know the current condition of your roof. This will help you plan for otherwise unforeseen circumstances such as leaks.

What are the benefits of getting your roof replaced?

There are many benefits to getting your roof replaced. If you are trying to sell your property, you can increase the value of it before sale. New roofs also help keep your house insulated. This means you end up paying less to keep your property cool in the summer and warm in the winter.

Why work with Orbital View?

When you work with us, you save yourself time and ensure the job is done correctly. With our proprietary insurance claim methods, we make sure that you get the most coverage possible to get the best roof you can. You work with the top roofers in the industry with the right insurance coverages in place to protect your roof. We understand how important a roof is to your property. Our goal is to deliver the best experience possible.

Is there any charge to get my roof assessed?

Currently, we are not charging for any remote roof assessments. This is something we are doing to spread awareness of our solution so take advantage of it while it lasts.

I have my owner roofer I want to work with, can you still help me?

The answer to this is an emphatic yes! If you have a roofer that you would like to use, that is fine as well. We help you through the insurance claim process to make sure you maximize your coverage. After the roof claim is approved, we work with your preferred roofer to get the job done.
